PB & J

Description

PB & Js are the go-to food for exhausted parents around the nation. In my opinion, they are a 5/10 food.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Put peanut butter on bread
  2. Put jelly on other slice
  3. Slap bread together
  4. Enjoy!
