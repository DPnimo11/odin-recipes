Applesauce

Description

Applesauce is a world renowned snack, particularly among elementary school children.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Fist

Steps

  1. Gather some apples
  2. Core the apples
  3. Punch the apples until desired consistency is reached
  4. Enjoy!
