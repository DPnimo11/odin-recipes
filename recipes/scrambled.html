Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled Eggs are like normal eggs, except scrambled.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Oil/butter
  • Seasoning
  • Optional: milk

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs until uniform consistency, adding milk if desired
  2. Warm skillet with olive oil or butter
  3. Add in eggs, using spatula to flip eggs every few seconds
  4. Once cooked, add salt and pepper as desired.
  5. Enjoy!
